Freezes the Nameless Deity boss in its idle animation and renders a solid
green screen background behind it. Useful for recording GIFs or footage.

The boss will play its intro cutscene fully so all parts load correctly,
then freeze in place with full animations running (wings, hands, vines).

Press F10 to toggle all in-game UI (hotbar, health bar, boss bar, etc.)
on and off for a clean recording frame.

Requires Calamity Mod.
